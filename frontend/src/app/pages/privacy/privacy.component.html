Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account we collect your email address and the display name you choose. When you report a sighting or register a dog we store the information you submit — including location coordinates, health notes, and any photos you upload. We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, or any government-issued identifiers.

2. How we use it

Your display name is shown publicly next to sightings you report so other community members know who filed them. Your email address is used only for authentication — it is never displayed publicly and we will not send you marketing emails. Location data is used solely to plot sighting trails on the map and help people find dogs in their area.

3. Photos

Photos you upload are stored on our server and are publicly accessible to anyone who visits a dog's profile page. Please do not upload photos that contain people's faces or any personally identifying information. By uploading a photo you confirm you have the right to share it.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. All data entered into PawID is used exclusively for the purpose of this community platform. We may share aggregated, anonymised statistics (such as total sighting counts per area) publicly, but these cannot be traced back to individual users.

5. Cookies and storage

PawID uses browser localStorage to keep you signed in between sessions. We do not use tracking cookies or any third-party analytics scripts. No data is shared with advertising networks.

6. Data retention

Your account and all associated sightings remain in our database until you request deletion. To delete your account and all data tied to it, contact us using the link in the footer. We will process the request within 7 days.

7. Security

Passwords are stored as BCrypt hashes — we never store them in plain text. Communication between your browser and the server uses HTTPS. While we take reasonable precautions, no system is perfectly secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of PawID after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
